About Us

Who we are

KKR Investments is a black South African limited liability investment company based in Johannesburg. The company's areas of Investment are Property, Agriculture, Aquaculture, ICT, and Manufacturing.

The company underpins its consulting arm, KKR Consulting, through which it undertakes public sector reform projects for national, provincial and local governments with the complement of dedicated sector specialists.

Our Vision

To be an enabler of social and economic reform through solutions we provide and to add value to initiatives geared towards a good quality of life for all South Africans.

Our Mission

To contribute to a properous South Africa by investing in acceptable human habitats and food security, and to be a platform that facilitates actionable decisions in support of the social and economictransformation programmes of Government.
